FAQ

1. How do I book a trip with RoamGO?
You can book a trip directly through our website. Just choose your destination,
select a package, and follow the easy booking steps. Need help? Our travel experts are,
just a message away
2. Can I customize my travel itinerary?
Absolutely! We offer fully customizable travel packages so you can explore at your own pace.
Add or remove destinations, choose your type of stay, and personalize your adventure.
3. What’s included in the tour packages?
Most of our packages include accommodation, transport, guided tours, meals, and entrance
fees to major attractions. Details are provided under each package.
4. Is international travel assistance available?
Yes! We offer 24/7 assistance for international travelers, including emergency support,
local guides, and help with documentation.
5. Do you help with visa processing?
We provide complete visa guidance and support for most countries. However, final approval
is subject to the embassy of the destination country.
6. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, and international payment gateways like PayPal.
7. Can I cancel or reschedule my trip?
Yes, our cancellation and rescheduling policies are traveler-friendly. You can cancel or change your plans based
on the package terms. Just check the policy before confirming your booking.
8. Are group discounts available?
Definitely! We offer special discounts for group bookings. The more, the merrier—and more affordable!
9. How safe is it to travel with RoamGO?
Your safety is our priority. We partner with trusted vendors, follow all travel regulations,
and offer support in case of emergencies.
10. Do you offer student or budget-friendly packages?
Yes! We have budget travel options, student-friendly tours,
and backpacker plans tailored for young, adventurous travelers.
